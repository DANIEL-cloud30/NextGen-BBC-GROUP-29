Our Story

Eco Strong Minds Shelf was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to create a world where sustainable cities thrive through the combined power of knowledge and wellness. We believe that books have the transformative ability to inspire change, while mindfulness practices nurture the mental resilience needed to build and maintain sustainable communities.

What started as a small community bookshelf in a local park has grown into a comprehensive platform connecting individuals, organizations, and communities around the shared goal of creating environmentally conscious, socially equitable, and mentally healthy urban spaces.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To empower individuals and communities with accessible resources that foster sustainable urban development and mental wellbeing through literature, education, and collaborative action.

Vision: A world where every city is a model of sustainability, where green spaces flourish, communities are connected, and mental health is prioritized alongside environmental stewardship.

Our Core Values

Sustainability
We champion environmentally responsible practices and long-term thinking in all our initiatives.
Knowledge Sharing
We believe in the democratization of knowledge and the power of education to drive change.
Community
We foster collaboration, inclusivity, and collective action in building sustainable futures.
Wellness
We integrate mental and emotional wellbeing as essential components of sustainable living.

Our Impact

Since our inception, we have:

  • Established 12 community bookshelves in urban neighborhoods
  • Hosted 50+ workshops on sustainable living practices
  • Connected 200+ community members through our wellness programs
  • Partnered with 15 local organizations to amplify our impact
